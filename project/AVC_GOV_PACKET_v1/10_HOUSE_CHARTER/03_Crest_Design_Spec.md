# House Crest – Design Spec

Shield:

- Quartered heater shield.

Quadrants:

1. Mountains of Exile – white peaks on deep blue (Piedmont → US).
2. Tower and Faultline – split tower over a faint skyline (pre/post 9/11).
3. Wheel and Wing – motorcycle wheel with a wing (Bronx shop × ballet).
4. Book and Circuit – open book turning into traces and code (archive × tech).

Center:

- Heart-shaped lock where quadrants meet; keyhole in the shape of a lowercase "a" (Alice/Alicia/Allison/Aurora).

Supporters:

- Left: betta fish with skirt-like fins (solitary, ritual separation, beauty with teeth).
- Right: fox with a ballet ribbon (clever survival, artistry, child-guard).

Crest:

- Laurel woven with small myth-tech glyphs.

Banner:

- “Memoria Custodit Veritatem”
- “I Carry The Record.”
