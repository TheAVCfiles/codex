# CT Victim Compensation — Draft Packet Text (Working Template)

> Status: Draft support text for form-filling. Not legal advice.

## A. Core Filing Components
- Main application form (injury category used by filer).
- Waiver request for late filing (if outside standard filing window).
- Supporting attachments list.

## B. Narrative Skeleton (Neutral)
"I am requesting victim compensation related to domestic-violence-associated harm and resulting economic disruption. The harm period includes coercive conditions, housing instability, and documented work/income disruption. I am attaching available records including messages, financial records, and supporting safety documentation."

## C. Good-Cause Waiver Skeleton
"I request waiver of filing timeliness due to trauma, safety concerns, and displacement conditions that delayed reporting and document assembly. The delay was not abandonment of claim; it reflects ongoing safety and stabilization barriers. Supporting records are attached."

## D. Emergency Flag Text (Cover Note)
Subject line example: `URGENT DV CRISIS — compensation filing + waiver request`

Body example:
"Please prioritize review of attached victim compensation packet and waiver request due to immediate safety/economic instability. A preliminary review or partial emergency consideration is requested if available under program procedures."

## E. Attachment Checklist
- [ ] Evidence ledger excerpt (IDs and short descriptions)
- [ ] Timeline excerpt (neutral timeline CSV/PDF)
- [ ] Safety/protective documentation (if available)
- [ ] Financial-impact documents (bank/broker/wage records)
- [ ] Communication exhibits (emails/messages) with visible timestamps

## F. Intake Safety Controls
- Use safe mailing address/PO Box where applicable.
- Redact minors' identifying information before submission.
- Preserve unredacted originals in `CASE_CAPSULE/evidence_raw/`.
