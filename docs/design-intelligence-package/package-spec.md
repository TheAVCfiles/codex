# Package Spec: Design Intelligence (Enterprise)

## Product definition

**Design Intelligence** is a repeatable service product that transforms complex organizational signals (strategy, UX, engineering, operations, and customer feedback) into **decision-grade design artifacts** and **implementation-ready guidance**.

## Core promise

- Reduce decision latency.
- Increase alignment across stakeholders.
- Provide defensible, auditable rationale for design direction.

## Inputs (required)

- Product brief or problem statement
- Current experience flows or UI inventory
- Research artifacts (if available)
- Constraints: legal, technical, brand, compliance
- Stakeholder list with decision owners

## Inputs (optional)

- Analytics snapshots or KPIs
- Customer support logs
- Competitive references

## Outputs (standard)

- Executive summary (1–2 pages)
- Decision map (dependencies and tradeoffs)
- Experience blueprint (current → target)
- Opportunity register with priority scoring
- Design system alignment memo
- Implementation brief (handoff to product/engineering)

## Outputs (enterprise add-ons)

- Governance model (roles, cadence, risk controls)
- Multi-quarter roadmap with confidence levels
- Policy and compliance traceability grid
- Change management brief

## Quality gates (non-negotiable)

- Clear problem framing and scope boundaries
- Traceable evidence for recommendations
- Explicit decision owners and approval steps
- Final deliverables signed off by an accountable sponsor

## Service levels

- **Standard**: 2–4 weeks, scoped to a single product area
- **Enterprise**: 6–10 weeks, multi-team alignment and governance
- **Retainer**: ongoing monthly audits + quarterly refresh

## Success criteria

- Stakeholder alignment achieved within two review cycles
- Recommendations mapped to constraints and risks
- Handoff package accepted without rework
